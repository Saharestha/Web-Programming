Donation Events

Pashupati Old Age Home

On 19th August 2016, we visited Old Age Home located in Pashupatinath Temple where we were able to donate different food items. They all were very welcoming and were very happy to see us. It was a nice time spending time and sharing stories with each other.


Orphange Donation

On 12th of nOvember 2016, we visited Orphange located in Budhanilkantha. The children in orphanage didn't have enough clothes to wear so we collected and donated clothes. We also played different games with thwm. Seeing smile on those faces gave us warmth and happiness.


Cleaning Events

School Surrounding

On 17th September 2016, we, in association with Clean Up Nepal, volunteered to clean the surrounding area of school. We along with cleaning spread awareness among people to keep their surrounding clean. We were successful in cleaning the area.


Festive Events

Deepawali Event

On the occassion of Deepawali, we with leo club of Mount Makalu organized a Deusi Bhailo event. Leos from different clubs came and performed in this event. We were also able to get some donation.


Dashain Event

On the occassion of Dashain, a major festival in Nepal, we organized this event in school. Many students from our talent showed off their talent. It was a very fun event overall.
